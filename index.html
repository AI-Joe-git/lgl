Our Process

Step 1: Notary Appointment

We will arrange a notary public appointment on your behalf to sign the necessary documents and power of attorney.

Step 2: Obtaining (NIF) & (NIE)

We will obtain a Spanish tax identification number (NIE) and (NIF) on your behalf, so you can start invoicing with your brand new Spanish company and account

Step 3: Bank Account Opening

We will assist you in opening a business bank account with BBVA or any other major Spanish bank, without you having to set foot in Spain.
